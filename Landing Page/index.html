About Us

Okailon is an e-sports team that was founded in 2018 by Based Jamali. Currently, Okailon has 20+ members worldwide. If you want to be a part of our team, fill out the application below! Everyone is welcome to join our team. At Okailon, no one is getting discriminated because of their skin color or appearance. As a team, we strive for success, dominance, and cohesion. And this has led us to win numerous tournaments and competitions:

1)  2019 -  Fortnite-Battleroyal Cup
2)  Feb. 2020 -  Apex-Legends Crown
3)  Jul. 2020 -  Valorent World Cup

4)  Dez. 2020 -  Rocket-League Title
5)  Jan. 2021 -  FIFA World Cup
6)  May 2021 -  Brawl Stars Euro Cup

7)  Mar. 2022 -  Clash-Royal World Cup / Clash of Clans World Cup
